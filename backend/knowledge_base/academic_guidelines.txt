Academic Calendar 2026:
The fall semester starts on August 24, 2026.
The mid-term exams will be held from October 12 to October 16, 2026.
The final semester exams are scheduled from December 7 to December 18, 2026.
Winter break begins on December 19, 2026, and classes resume on January 11, 2027.

Attendance Policy:
Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible to appear for the final semester examinations.
Any student with attendance between 65% and 74% due to medical reasons must submit an official medical certificate signed by a registered doctor within 3 days of returning to campus.
Students with attendance below 65% will be automatically debarred from the course examinations.

Placement Guidelines:
Placements for the final year graduating students will begin on September 7, 2026.
To register with the placement cell, a student must have a minimum Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of 6.0 and zero active backlogs.
Students are allowed a maximum of 3 job offers, after which they are automatically opted out of the placement season.
The highest package offered last year was 45 LPA, with an average package of 8.5 LPA.

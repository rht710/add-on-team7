College Tuition Fees and Scholarship Guidelines:

Standard Tuition Fees structure for all branches:
- Engineering (B.Tech / B.E.): 1,20,000 INR per semester.
- Computer Applications (BCA / MCA): 80,000 INR per semester.
- Management (BBA / MBA): 95,000 INR per semester.
- Sciences (B.Sc / M.Sc): 50,000 INR per semester.

Scholarship Programs Based on Marks / Performance:
The college offers merit-based fee concessions and scholarships to encourage academic excellence. Concessions are categorized into groups based on performance in qualifying exams (such as 12th board exams for undergraduates or graduation CGPA/percentage for postgraduates):

1. Platinum Scholars Group (Marks: 95% and above or CGPA 9.5 and above):
- Tuition Fee Concession: 100% tuition fee waiver.
- Criteria: Must maintain a CGPA of 9.0 or above in subsequent semesters.
- Benefits: Free access to premium campus research labs and priority registration for electives.

2. Golden Scholars Group (Marks: 90% to 94.9% or CGPA 9.0 to 9.49):
- Tuition Fee Concession: 50% tuition fee waiver.
- Criteria: Must maintain a CGPA of 8.5 or above in subsequent semesters.
- Benefits: Access to specialized industry certification programs at no additional cost.

3. Silver Scholars Group (Marks: 80% to 89.9% or CGPA 8.0 to 8.99):
- Tuition Fee Concession: 25% tuition fee waiver.
- Criteria: Must maintain a CGPA of 8.0 or above in subsequent semesters.

4. Bronze Scholars Group (Marks: 75% to 79.9% or CGPA 7.5 to 7.99):
- Tuition Fee Concession: 10% tuition fee waiver.
- Criteria: Must maintain a CGPA of 7.5 or above in subsequent semesters.

Additional Scholarships:
- Sports Scholarship: 15% tuition fee waiver for state-level representatives and 30% waiver for national-level representatives.
- Need-Based Financial Aid: Up to 50% waiver for students with annual family income less than 2,50,000 INR, subject to document verification by the college administrative board.
